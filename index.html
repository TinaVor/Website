About the studio

Since 2006, I have been dealing with interiors not only for residential premises, but also for commercial ones. I turn repairs from a problem into tasks that we solve together with the team.

Interiors are a sought-after area. A high-quality and professional project is developed for members of a particular family or company, taking into account personal habits, tastes and individual characteristics.

“We carry out works of any complexity. For the result to surpass even the most bold expectations!”

Olga Mudryakova, Studio Founder
Apartment design in Primorsky Kvartal residential complex
A small apartment in the residential complex Primorsky Quarter is designed for a woman, so the whole space was built for maximum convenience and comfort for one person. The apartment turned out to be quite minimalistic, built on a combination of natural materials, shades and textures.
House design in KP Repino Park
This is not the first experience of working with a customer. this time we worked on the interior of the living room and kitchen. There were no special stylistic wishes. But the house itself marked the vector of movement towards Scandinavian minimalism and asceticism.
One-room apartment in Promenade residential complex
The minimalistic interior is built on a combination of natural materials, an abundance of wood, imitation of different stones. According to the design of the space, the main wish of the customers was to make the apartment dark and warm.
Design of a two-room apartment in Symbol residential complex
The interior turned out to be rich in details, textures, color accents. Thanks to the abundance of wood and warm shades of background coatings and textiles, the interior turned out to be cozy and warm. But not boring by incorporating deep, dark shades of walls and furniture.
Design of a three-room apartment in the residential complex Svetlana Park
The interior of the room is dictated by the architecture of the house itself. The emphasis is primarily on the functionality of the layout, on the play of light and air, on the simplicity of forms and textures.
Design of a three-room apartment in residential complex Imperial
The family currently lives in an apartment with a combined space, but with the advent of the child, there was a desire to still have more separated areas for living. Therefore, in the new apartment, only the kitchen and the living room remained as united spaces, which was supposed to be the original layout.
Living Room for the Housing Issue
In recent years, under the influence of various difficult events, we are increasingly turning to nature in the desire to change the bustle of the city for places where you can be in greater solitude and harmony with yourself. We strive to surround ourselves with an eco-friendly interior not only from an aesthetic point of view, but also from a consumer point of view, focusing on natural materials.
Design of a small house 3x7 in the Scandinavian style
The main objective of the project was to develop a typical interior for a 20 sq.m house for rent in the Leningrad Region. As in any other type of rental housing, we started from the goals in commercial market research, selected wear-resistant materials within the specified budget.
